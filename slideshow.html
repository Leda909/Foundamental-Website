Mandalas

A mandala is a spiritual and ritual symbol. There are various types of mandalas, but each built on a simetric structure and used for artistically and spiritually purposes as well.

The circle is the symbol of being whole and its' division and forms are the specialized meaning of them. Motives drawn in a specific order within the circle radiate joy, peace, harmony, tranquility and concentration.
